Matogen Celebrates operational years, founded in 2007

At Matogen, our commitment lies in crafting comprehensive solutions, not just software.

We are a dedicated and driven team that passionately believes in developing tools to address real-life challenges and assist individuals and businesses in achieving their objectives, for customers around the globe.

Our primary focus is collaborating with forward-thinking companies to foster their growth. By delivering superior and exceptional web and cloud solutions, we aim to support our clients' business needs and propel them to new levels of excellence.

Industries of expertise

Finance
Driven by innovation, we develop cutting-edge software empowering financial institutions to optimize operations, enhance security, and elevate customer experiences.
Education
Empowering education through innovative software solutions. Revolutionizing learning for success in the digital age.
Mining
By utilizing advanced technologies such as data analytics, artificial intelligence, and automation, we empower mining companies to enhance efficiency, safety, and sustainability in their operations.
Manufacturing
We specialize in tailored solutions across supply chain, quality control, and more. Through AI, IoT, and data analytics, we enhance manufacturing efficiency and decision-making.
Agriculture
Committed to agriculture, we combine traditional practices with tech-driven, efficient, and eco-conscious processes, bolstering global food security.
Transport Telemetry
Leveraging state-of-the-art technologies, we offer a comprehensive suite of tools that cater to diverse transport modes, from logistics and public transit to commercial and private fleets.
Tourism
Fusing industry insight with technology, we craft tailored software empowering tourism businesses in the digital age—streamlining operations, enhancing engagement, and enabling unforgettable journeys.
Telecoms
Combining technical expertise with deep telecom knowledge, we create tailored software solutions that boost efficiency, elevate customer experiences, and drive clients to the forefront of technology.
